---- # About us etki Turkish Tech is an online community of turkish speaking technologists abroad. Founded in 2016 with the name of _Turkish Tech Berlin_, started as a local meetup group for exchanging and networking purposes among the tech community in Berlin, Germany. Currently etki TurkishTech is an international online community built for cooperation and exchange between turkish speaking tech experts with migration background, also including technologists from other nationalities.
